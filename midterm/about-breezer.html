About Breezer

Breezer is a time reminder for you to take intervals of short breaks from the computer. Work on a task until the timer ends and take a breather!


A technique by Francesco Cirillo have shown that 25 minutes intervals are the optimum time to increase productivity and a great way to decrease fatigue.


Why use Breezer?

Audio notification, desktop notification and encouragement throughout countdown to motivate you.
