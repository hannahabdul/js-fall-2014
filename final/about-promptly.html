Promptly.




Promptly is a virtual journal for you to enjoy the little things in life.


This concept is based on traditional journaling methods but with more interactive digital interface with simple prompt cards designed to keep you on your feet. Little prompts such as "Treat yourself to something sweet" or "Write about something you didn't do this weekend" provide ways that allows for self reflection and rethinking the mundane activities.
